# Wemo — Project Reference File

## Main Mission

Wemo mainly exists for businesses that don't have any online presence — helping them get found and booked. It also helps tourists, but the core mission is giving offline businesses a place online. No size limit on which businesses qualify — small family-run spots and bigger local businesses both fit, as long as they're not online yet.

Positioning goal: Wemo should feel like a "must have" app for anyone on vacation in Georgia — the reason it feels essential is being one place instead of juggling many different sites and social media pages to find and book things.

Main users: mainly tourists, with locals as a secondary bonus audience.

## Deployment Plan

- Now: a simple mobile-friendly website using plain HTML/CSS, hosted on GitHub Pages.
- Eventually: native iOS and Android applications.
- Current status: homepage beta mockup only.
- Goal: validate the idea before building the full application.

## Business Model

- Listings are free during launch.
- Future monetization may include subscriptions or booking commissions.
- Businesses can onboard themselves for free.
- Premium onboarding includes professionally built profiles by Dav.

## MVP Scope

Included:
- Business profile page
- Booking request form (email-based)
- Category and location filtering
- Businesses posting their own events and deals

Not included:
- Reviews
- User accounts
- Payment processing

Design direction: cinematic hero with clean, simple UX.

## What to Avoid

- Generic template feeling
- Slow loading
- Poor mobile experience
- Empty-looking business profiles
- Overcomplicated UX

## Success Criteria

Launch with 5–10 real businesses before focusing on polish.

## Language & Market

- English + Georgian
- Launch city: Batumi
- Kobuleti follows
- Domain not yet purchased

## Go-To-Market

- Validate in person during the Summer 2026 Batumi trip.
- Then expand using TikTok, Instagram and Facebook.

## Validation Gate

The Summer 2026 research trip determines whether the project continues or is cancelled based on business interest.

1. Free/simple solutions first.
